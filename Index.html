FaQs

Can you use it when Electricity cuts off ?

Yes, you can use it even if Electricity cuts off. We provide you another switch which uses rechargable batteries. By turning on this switch, Your windows will work efficiently.

Can you open the window manually after installing our system ?

No, you cannot open the windows manually. We will give you the switch or button, you can open or close the windows with that button. You can open and close the windows completely or partially.

For how much period, our system can run efficiently?

All the products used in the system are of good quality and high efficiency. The actuator (main device which open and closes the window) gives the osciallations of approx 50,000 times which can be last for approx 5 to 6 years.

At what speed of rain or air, the system will work?

The system will definitely work depending on the user's choice and where the user's home is. If user wants to operate the system when rain or air speed is very low/high, then we can make such system also.
